## How to acquire a word

1. basic (must do)
   1. 1st glance
      1. pronounce
      2. word frequency
      3. mean frequency
   2. 2nd glance
      1. mean list
      2. variants
         1. complex
         2. tense
         3. comparative
      3. associated words
         1. word family
         2. derivatives
         3. synonyms
         4. antonyms
   3. learning usage
      1. literal translation
      2. example sentences
      3. compositional phrasemes
2. advanced (optional and inefficient)
   1. paraphrase in 2nd language
   2. idioms
      1. weak idiom
      2. semi-idiom
      3. full idiom
   3. morpheme
      1. root
      2. affix
   4. etymology
